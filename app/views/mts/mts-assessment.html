Notification

Annual assessment reminder

Published 30 May 2022

You will be suspended if you do not pass your Group A annual assessment before the end of the training year. The training year begins on 1 April and ends on 31 March each year.

To pass your assessment, you must score at least 80% and upload it to your MTS profile.

If you do not pass it before the deadline, you cannot resume testing until you:

  • complete the assessment for the next training year
  • take a demonstration test with a DVSA vehicle examiner

Contact your assessment provider if your pass certificate is not showing on your MTS profile.
